Projects

DevGlobe
A Group Project using the MEAN Stack where I heavily worked in backend. DevGlobe uses D3.js to display past DevMountain students' "impact on the world" by mapping the locations of their jobs found after DevMountain on an interactive globe. In addition, we compared the costs of a typical 4 year University and other bootcamps to show how much DevMountain's courses can save students on average.
Divinity Salon
A static website created and customized for Divinity Salon located in Encinitas, California. This clean, simplistic website perfectly showcases Divinity Salon's minimalistic style and class. This design was created to be mobile and desktop friendly.
Appointment Scheduler
A Personal Project using the MEAN Stack, JavaScript FullCalendar, and D3.js intended for a salon with a team of stylists to schedule their appointments and store past client information. Includes a "timecard" feature for employees' hours and an employee stats page that uses D3.js to display rankings of employees based on customer reviews.
